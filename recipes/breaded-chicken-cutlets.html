Breaded Chicken Cutlets

Description

Breaded Chicken Cutlets are the perfect meal for family and friends because it can be served in so many different ways.

Ingredients

  • Boneless, Skinless, Chicken Breast Tenderloins
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Vegetable Oil
  • Lemon Wedges
  • Panko
  • Parmesan
  • All-Purpose Flour
  • Paprika
  • Eggs
  • Garlic Clove

Steps

  1. Cover chicken with plastic wrap and pound chicken to even thickness.
  2. Distribute into three shallow dishes, panko bread crumbs and parmesan in one, flour and paprika in another, and crack eggs into the other one with garlic.
  3. One cutlet at a time, dredge in flour and then shake off excess. Next, dredge in eggs, and allow excess to drip off. Lastly, dredge in panko crumbs, press gently to make sure crumbs stick to the chicken.
  4. Place chicken on a wire rack that is sprayed with a non-stick spray. Let chicken rest for 5 minutes.
  5. Cook cutlets in the oil two at a time, for about 2-3 minutes. Then flip cutlets, reduce heat to medium, and cook until cutlets are a deep golden brown, and crisp.
  6. Remove chicken cutlets from oil, and place on a paper towel lined plate.
  7. Serve chicken with lemon wedges.
