Kraft Macaroni and Cheese

Description

A childhood favorite that has a nostalgic feeling, there's nothing quite like the original!

Ingredients

  • One 7.5 oz box of Original Kraft Macaroni & Cheese
  • 6 cups of water
  • 2 tablespoons of butter
  • 1/4 cup of milk

Steps

  1. Boil 6 cups of water and add macaroni, cook for 8 mins, stir occasionally.
  2. Drain macaroni, add butter into pot, pour cooked macaroni on top, stir to coat macaroni in butter.
  3. Sprinkle powdered cheese across all macaroni, stir, add milk, stir until all ingredients are fully incorporated.
